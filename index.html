How it works

Connect your services

Authenticate with Apple Music, Spotify, and others using secure tokens.

Select playlists

Review & confirm

Check the matches, make edits if you like, then transfer with one tap.

FAQ

Do you store my music library?
No. We read metadata to perform transfers and keep only what’s required temporarily to complete the task.
Which services are supported?
Apple Music and Spotify at launch, with others to follow.
